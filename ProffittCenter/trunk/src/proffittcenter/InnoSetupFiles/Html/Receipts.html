Receipts

Receipts are only printed after the sale is complete. When a receipt is required, press the Print Screen key - the one labelled 'Print/SysRq' on most keyboards, at any time after the first item is sold, up until the sales screen has cleared. A receipt is printed out on the receipt printer selected in the  screen. . A receipt for a past sale can be printed by locating the sale in the Tables>Sales or Tables>Sale screen and pressing the Print Screen key or clicking the Printer button. If you require a receipt for the last sale, after the screen has cleared, then press the back arrow key which will show the last sale on screen, and then print from this.

The normal mode of operation is with a receipt only being printed on demand. If, however, a receipt is required after every sale, there is a check box option for this choice in the Settings screen on the Shop tab.

There is also an option to print receipts whenever the sales total exceeds a fixed amount. Enter the amount in pounds in the box adjacent to the 'Receipts always' check box in the Settings screen on the Shop tab.

The layout of receipts can be altered somewhat. Here is the procedure for setting it up for a correctly formatted receipt.

Go to File>Settings and choose the Hardware tab.

Press the test button next to Printer port and count number of characters on receipt.

Enter number of characters in appropriate box.

Click the green arrow. The program will shut down.

Start program again. Print out a receipt from past sales. 

You can adjust heading spacing in File>Settings and choose the Hardware tab. That is the line -

 'Qty Product Price Charge'.
