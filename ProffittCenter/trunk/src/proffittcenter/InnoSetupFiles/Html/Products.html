Products

The Products table is shown by selecting the menu item Table>Products.

A table of all products in the system is shown, in barcode order. You can order them by description or by department by clicking on the appropriate column headers.

To find further details of a particular product, select the product with your mouse and then click the Details button which will show the Product screen.

If you select a product and then click  Sell, or just press the Enter key, the product will be sold, just as though you scanned it.

Dismiss the screen by clicking the Close button with a small marked x, or by pressing the Escape key.

There is also a button  for printing a barcode on a dedicated barcode printer. This can be used with a multiple  selection if required.
