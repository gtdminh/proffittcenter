Going further

Now that we have rushed through a sale or two - feel free to do more, or add further products either using different hot keys or typing or scanning in the full bar code for any available product - it is time to explore the system a bit more.

Try deleting a product sale before it has been paid for: just press the delete key if it is the last item sold, or if not, select the desired line with your mouse before pressing delete.

Select the menu item Tables, and then choose Products to see the stored products so far.

Now press the escape key to dismiss this form, and select the Tables>Sales>All menu selection, to examine past sales.

Now try a stock take screen, say the menu selection Stock>Stock take>All, and you will see a list of products needing a stock checked. Any items entered today will not appear, as it assumed that the correct stock level was entered when you created the product entry. The stock level for any item can be amended by double clicking on the stock quantity column, and over typing.

Feel free to examine further aspects of the system. Try selecting Reports|Takings and clicking the Calculate button to see the takings so far today! Business is looking good. And so is your shop.
