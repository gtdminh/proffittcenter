Hot keys

Any single letter key in the range a to z, (or, alternatively, letters in the range A to Z,) is used to ease entry for common products. Products sold by weight or count, such as greengrocery or penny sweets, can be assigned to a hot key with a price of just one penny. The letter a key is allocated for zero rate VAT sales and the letter b key to standard rate VAT sales. The letter u key is allocated to Agency sales such as PayPoint.

The letter a or A is converted into a code of 1000001 and the letter z or Z to 1000026, with appropriate values for intermediate letters. Hot keys can be created, just as other products, by using these numbers instead of barcodes. They can also be created in the Starting stock screen or the Delivery entry screen.
