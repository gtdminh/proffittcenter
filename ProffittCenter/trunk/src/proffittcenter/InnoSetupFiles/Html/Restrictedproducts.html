Restricted products

Product can be restricted either by setting an age limit or by a pharmacist only requirement. A restriction is set in place by imposing the restriction on the department the product is in. For age restrictions, set the age in the restriction field of the department - i.e. 18 for a minimum age of 18. For pharmacist only put a letter p in the field. Tracked products should have a letter t inserted.

When a restricted product is sold, a question is asked via the screen, and the operator responds with a y for yes. Any other key press is treated as a no, and the sale is not completed.

If an age related product is sold, the system will not ask again for the same customer.
