Selling more than one

Now, let us increase the number of items sold from one to five. Type *5 followed by enter, just as you might on a calculator. The number sold will increase to five. (Note, that this is not really like a calculator as if we follow this action by typing *4, the quantity will become 4 and not 20) You can also increase this by one more just by pressing the Enter key; the quantity will increase by one each time you do this, just like on your present cash register. You could also combine two steps into o

ne by typing i5  followed by the Tab key, to save time.

Additional products can also be sold. Try typing p20 for 20p worth of penny sweets, and follow the same steps to create a new product as before.
