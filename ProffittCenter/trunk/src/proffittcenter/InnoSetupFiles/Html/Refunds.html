Refunds

If a customer brings back an item for a refund and it is decided to repay the customer, then type a minus sign, -, and then scan the item. Alternatively, the item can be scanned first and then the minus sign pressed followed by the Enter or Tab key. Every item in a sale can be returned at one go by typing - (minus) followed by the Enter key whilst holding the Shift key down. The amount to be paid will be shown as negative, indicating a refund. Entering a zero followed by the enter key will then open the cash drawer. All operators can perform this action. Refunds can be performed as part of a sale, if desired, such as when items are exchanged. After these operations are complete, the item will have been returned to stock.
