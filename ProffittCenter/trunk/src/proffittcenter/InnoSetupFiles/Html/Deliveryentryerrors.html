Delivery entry errors

All of the entries in Delivery entry can be changed, such as Pack size, Pack price, Product price, and current Stock quantity. The values are not updated until the OK (green tick) button is clicked. Number of packs can be set to zero to avoid entering additional stock. However, if you make a mistake on entry, this will not correct this mistake. Say that you have packs of 24 Coca Cola, but you mistakenly leave the Pack size at 1, then in addition to having the wrong pack size, you will have the wrong number in stock. What you would need to do is to deliver another 23 single cans and then change the Pack size to 24 and deliver 0 of these. This would leave all prices, margins and stock levels correct.

As an alternative problem, if you had mistakenly changed the Pack size from 1 to 9, you would have delivered 8 to many, so in Delivery entry change the Pack size to 1 and deliver -8.

Wholesale and retail prices can be corrected in the Delivery screen. Say we had accepted a delivery of Coca Cola cans but entered a price of 69p instead of Â£6.95. Then in Tables>Deliveries, select the Delivery and and then click the Information button. If you are not sure which Delivery was involved, use the search box: type in a part of the product name (coca), and press Enter. The last delivery will be highlighted.

The Information button will give the Delivery screen. In this, select the line in error, and then click the Change pack price button and alter the price.

The Pack record can also be altered by going to Tables>Packs, searching for Coca Cola using the Product search box, clicking on the Information button and then making changes.
