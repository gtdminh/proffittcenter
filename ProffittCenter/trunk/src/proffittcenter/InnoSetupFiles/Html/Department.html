Department

The Department screen is displayed from Departments either by clicking the 'New' button or by selecting a department and clicking the 'Modify' button.

The first field is the department description, a field that is capitalised after stepping on with the Tab key. Next is a tax drop-down box. Choose the default tax for this department using the arrow keys. Tab to the next field and select the default supplier for goods in this department. The next field, 'Default margin', is the expected percentage on return (por) for this range of goods. The default minimum specifies a minimum stock level, but leave this as zero for best use of the system.

Restriction is either an age, say 18 for alcohol, or the letter P for pharmacist permission with UK law. Click the OK button to accept the new or changed department or use the Escape key to dismiss the screen.
