Cashup reconciliation

Selecting Reports>Cashup reconciliation will bring up a  list of the ID's of Cashups that have not been reconciled. Choose one and then click OK, to bring up the Cashup reconciliation screen for that cashup.

Here you can start by reconciling the debits, cheques, coupons, received on account and paid outs against the paper record. Any unresolved discrepancy can be entered in the total boxes below the columns.

Then, starting with Â£50 notes (subject to currency of course), enter the count of each note or coin in the appropriate  location, tabbing from space to space. (Shift Tab will take you back one step) When you have entered the figures for all of the cash in the cash drawer, click on the Calculate button. Then click on the Calculate float button.

The net error is shown on the screen. The error will be positive if the is too much cash in the cash drawer. The paper document associated with debits, cheques, etc. should then be reconciled. If for any of these, reconciliation proves impossible, the total figure for the item should be altered, so that at least the error is recorded.

When complete, click the Save button to save the reconciled cashup in the database. Reconciled cashups can be viewed later from the Tables menu.
