Paying for it

Now to accept payment! Assume that the customer offers one pound in payment. Type 100 followed by Tab key. Money values are always entered in pence, with ProffittCenter. The money is accepted, and the change calculated. Your first sale is completed. If you have opted in Settings for a receipt every time, and you have a receipt printer, a receipt will be printed. If not, just press the Print Screen key on your keyboard.
