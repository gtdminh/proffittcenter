Multipacks

There is sometimes a need to have multipacks and single packs linked to a single SKU. If this is not done, then it is necessary to book out the multipack and treat the individual items as a fresh delivery each time a multipack is split for resale.

Let us for example consider Stella Artois cans sold singly at £1 each. You could also sell whole cases (24 cans) at £22 a case. The case would be recognised by the case barcode. We are not discussing offers here. The singles and cases are treated as different products being sold at different prices. What we require is for the stock level of Stella cans to be reduced by 24 every time a case is sold.

This is achieved by changing the multipack quantity from its' default value of 1 to 24 in the New Product dialog. As this makes no sense, unless the product is linked to an existing product, a link must also be made in the SKU drop down box before this can be effected.

Note that when stocktaking, it is now the number of cans of Stella that will be counted, even if whole cases are held.
