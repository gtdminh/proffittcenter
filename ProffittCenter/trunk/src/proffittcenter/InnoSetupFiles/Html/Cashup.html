Cashup

On selecting Reports>Cashing up, a display of key figures is shown since the last cashing up. These are the total Takings, Charges, Agency sales, and the totals for Coupons, Debits and Paid outs. In addition, the sum of Accounts charged is shown. These figures will include any tax taken. In addition, the start and end date and time for the cashing up period (Starting from the last time and ending now), and the operator who performed the cashing up. On clicking OK, the program closes down. To reconcile the takings, the cash, debits,coupons and Paid outs in the till should equal to the Takings figure less the Charges plus any Agency sales.  The figure for the takings for accounting purposes is taken from Takings screen for the given period. The takings are counted and reconciled using the  screen.
